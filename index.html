About Me

Welcome! I am a fourth-year PhD candidate in Mathematics at Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Yifeng Liu. I am currently a visiting student at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (2025–2026).

I received my B.S. in Mathematics from Peking University (2018–2022).

My CV is available here.

I am interested in both research mathematics and formalized mathematics. In particular, I enjoy using Lean4 to formalize olympiad and undergraduate-level problems.

Research Interests

Algebraic number theory, automorphic representation theory, the classical Langlands program, and arithmetic theta lifting for unitary groups.
